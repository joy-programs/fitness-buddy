ℹ️ About Habit Tracking

Building small daily habits is the most powerful way to achieve fitness goals. Track these 5 keystone habits every day:

  • 🏋️ Workout — Any exercise counts: home workout, walk, yoga
  • 💧 Water Goal — Aim for at least 2 litres daily
  • 🥗 Healthy Meal — Eat one mindful, nutritious meal
  • 😴 Good Sleep — 7-8 hours of quality sleep
  • 🚶 Daily Movement — A short walk, stretch, or movement break
💡 Consistency beats intensity. Doing something small every day beats an intense session once a week.
